About Us

Our company MindfulBits has developed a mental health and wellness application to help optimize performance and recovery for athletes participating in the 2030 Winter Olympics. Our app is loaded with an ecentric array of elements to make spending time for your mental health both worthwhile and convenient. By offering “Mind Bites” unique to each user throughout the day, we make prioritizing your mental health accessible despite busy schedules. We know that making-time is probably one the biggest challenges anyone faces throughout their day, this is why MindfulBITS will merge multiple facets of mental health support in one application to cater your needs.
