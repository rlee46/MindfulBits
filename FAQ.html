FAQ'S

Why is mental health important?

In very simple terms, mental health affects how we as individuals think, feel and act throughout our daily lives. It also aids in determining how well we handle stress, maintain our relationships, and make decisions.

How long should I spend on my mental health?

There's no set time as to how long you should spend on your mental health. Sometimes one may need to spend an entire day to settle their mind, or they may need to take a quick five-minute breather. Either way, it is recommended that you spend some time working on your mental health throughout the day to ensure overall wellness over long periods of time.

Why does a sportsman/woman need to work on their mental health?

Working on mental health shouldn't be ranked in importance according to profession, but when talking about sports one's mental wellness is quite underated. As we know that our mental health essentially affects the way we interact and function throughout the day. So it is very important that athletes practice their mental health on a daily basis to ensure success in their profession.
